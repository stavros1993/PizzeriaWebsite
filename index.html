Welcome to Pizza World!

Our pizzeria has been successfully owned and operated since 2005 by our family and welcomes you to drop by and join the locals in our fun and friendly atmosphere. We are located near the historical center, next to the metro station.

Our goal is to offer great and healthy homemade food at a reasonable price while maintaining great quality and service. Enjoy a selection of appetizers, pizza and spaghetti, including everything from classic favorites to modern cuisine..

Come join us for lunch or dinner and dine on our outdoor patio or in the newly remodeled dining room.
